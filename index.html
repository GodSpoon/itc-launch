About Us: Your Technology Partners

Located in Huntington, Indiana, Integrated Technology Consultants (ITC) specializes in helping people of all abilities use technology to live more freely and independently. We are experts in assessing your needs and recommending smart home devices, remote support tools, and other assistive technologies that fit your unique life.

Our core mission is to simplify daily life and enhance fulfillment by matching the right technology to your specific requirements and goals. We don't just install devices; we provide the consultation and guidance needed to ensure you or your clients can confidently use these tools. We partner with individuals, service providers, schools, and builders to create effective and dependable technology setups through careful planning and consultation.

Our Consulting Services

Assistive Technology Consulting & Setup

We provide expert advice, selection, and setup assistance for technology that empowers individuals, especially those with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities (IDD). Our consulting services cover:

  • Guidance on user-friendly, voice-controlled smart home systems.
  • Consultation on safety and remote support systems, helping you understand options for independent monitoring.
  • Identifying and implementing tech to aid with daily tasks and cognitive support.
  • Thorough needs assessment and creating a personalized technology integration plan.

Note: We consult on and help implement these technologies. We do not provide direct 24/7 monitoring services or active care ourselves but empower you or your chosen caregivers with the right tools.

IT Infrastructure Consulting

We also offer comprehensive IT consulting for organizations, ensuring robust and reliable technology foundations. This includes:

  • Design and consultation for reliable internet and Wi-Fi networks.
  • Technology planning and integration consulting for educational settings.
  • Advising on system maintenance strategies and support planning.
  • Consultation on secure access control for buildings and data protection strategies.
