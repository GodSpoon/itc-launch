About Us

Based in Huntington, Indiana, Integrated Technology Consultants is dedicated to empowering individuals of all abilities through technology. We specialize in seamlessly integrating smart home, remote monitoring, and assistive technologies into support systems.

Our mission is to enable more independent and fulfilled lives by bridging the gap between technological capabilities and unique individual needs. We work closely with service providers, schools, home builders, and other organizations to implement effective and reliable technology solutions.
