Your Technology Partners

Located in Huntington, Indiana, Integrated Technology Consultants (ITC) specializes in helping people of all abilities use technology to live more freely and independently. We are experts in assessing your needs and recommending smart home devices, remote support tools, and other assistive technologies that fit your unique life.

Our core mission is to simplify daily life and enhance fulfillment by matching the right technology to your specific requirements and goals. We don't just install devices; we provide the consultation and guidance needed to ensure you or your clients can confidently use these tools.
